About the gym

Built by a boxer.
Built for everyone.

First State Boxing is a community gym in Camden, Delaware built for people who want to learn, compete, get fit, and grow. We welcome all ages and all skill levels — from 7-year-olds stepping into a gym for the first time to adults looking for a real challenge.

Every member is part of the family from day one. No judgment. No ego. Just hard work and real coaching.

Coach Ant — Founder & Head Coach

Coach Ant has been in boxing for over 20 years — competing, sparring, and learning the sport from the inside out. What kept pulling him toward coaching wasn't just the boxing. It was the transformation he kept seeing in people.

He's developed fighters at every level — from complete beginners to amateur competitors, Olympians, and pros. He opened First State Boxing to bring that same coaching to Camden: a place where kids build confidence, discipline, and self-defense alongside their skills, and where adults get a real, intense workout and actually learn the sport.

Anyone who walks through the door gets taken to the next level — not just in the ring, but in life.
